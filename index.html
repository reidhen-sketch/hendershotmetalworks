Hendershot Metal Works is a full-service metal fabrication company based in Georgia. We combine precision engineering with creative design to deliver custom metalwork that meets exact specifications and exceeds expectations.

Founded by Reid Hendershot, our shop is equipped with professional-grade fabrication equipment including MIG/TIG welding stations, plasma cutting systems, and precision bending tools.
